OUR HAM STATIONS

Day Breakers


The perfect way to start the day! Our breakfast concept includes cooked-to-order eggs, omelet bar, pancakes, waffles, delicious breakfast proteins, and a selection of breakfast sandwiches.

Main Plate


A wide selection of classic favorites, trendy gourmet selections, and specialty sandwiches.

Bravo!


Bravo! brings the show to the dining room. Colorful arrays of fresh ingredients share center stage while our chefs showcase their talents as they quickly prepare each dish to order right in front of our guests’ eyes.

J. Clark’s Grille


Featuring house-made specialty burgers, hot sandwiches fresh from the grill, wings, fries, and more.

Green Scene


Fresh and flavorful fruits and vegetables are the starting point as our chefs created the tasteful Green Scene salad bar concept. Try our house-made, scratch dressings, and freshly prepared salads.

DuJour


When we say soup of the day, we mean it! Our soups are made fresh daily and the menu changes with the seasons because we use fresh, local ingredients ​whenever possible.

Villa Toscana


Made-to-order Italian specialties including hand prepared stromboli and pizza, along with fresh pasta.


ABOUT METZ

Metz Culinary Management is proud to bring New College the best on-campus food program with a variety of tastes that are sure to please.


The idea of living well is infused through the Metz experience. When you step into our dining locations, you will see it in our locally sourced ingredients, our fresh and flavorful menus, our emphasis on scratch prepared foods, and our friendly and personal service.
Our goal is to create a superior program for you.


BOAR'S HEAD



Visit our Market Street Deli, featuring Boar’s Head & C-Store items, for the hot, toasted sub sandwiches, flat bread pizzas, made-to-order deli sandwiches, delicious panini sandwiches featuring your choice of oven gold turkey, Black Forest ham, seasoned roast beef or veggie. Also featuring Peet’s coffee and tea, along with fountain beverages from Coca-Cola.
